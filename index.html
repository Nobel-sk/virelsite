About Virel

Virel is a student-led startup based in Ghana, passionate about making powerful AI writing tools accessible and affordable to everyone.

We believe that great communication should not be limited by cost or complexity. Our mission is to empower students, professionals, creators, and everyday users with AI that helps them express ideas clearly and confidently.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Virel?

Virel is an AI-powered writing assistant that helps you create, rewrite, and improve text quickly and effectively.

Is Virel free?

Yes! Virel offers a generous free plan with daily limits. Paid plans unlock higher usage and premium features.

What can I use Virel for?

Social media captions, school essays, emails, business writing, creative content, and any text improvement.

How does the AI work?

Virel uses advanced large language models trained on vast amounts of text to understand and generate human-like content.

Is my data safe?

Yes. We do not store your prompts long-term and use industry-standard encryption. Your privacy is important to us.
